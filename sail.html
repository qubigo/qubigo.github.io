Qubigo at Sail Amsterdam 2025

Qubigo is a strategic game designed for 2 players, where the goal is to create a row of 3 pieces with a shared characteristic. It can be played on different levels of complexity.

Order the Dutch Edition

Players must develop a 3D strategy while preventing their friends from scoring points. With just 27 moves to victory, Qubigo is a game that requires a sharp mind and calculated risk-taking.
